Do either (or both) of the problems about the free group on \(n\) generators denoted \[ F_n = \langle g_1, g_2, \dots, g_n \rangle \] which is a group presentation with no relations. For example, if \(n = 2\), then an example element of \(F_2 \cong \langle a, b \rangle\) is \(a^3b^{-1}a^{-4}b^{-1}a^2\).

  2. [5] In this part, you'll study some subgroups and quotient groups of this group, which might not be as hard as you expect!
    1. Write some elements of the cyclic subgroup \(H = \langle ab \rangle \leq F_2\).
    2. Is \(H \triangleleft F_2\)? Why or why not?
    3. Consider the map \(\varphi \colon F_2 \to D_3\) by \(\varphi(a) = r\) and \(\varphi(b) = f\). Is this map a homomorphism? An isomorphism?
    4. What is the image of \(\varphi\)?
    5. Without explicitly describing \(\ker(\varphi)\), what does the first isomorphism have to say about the quotient group \(F_2/\ker(\varphi)\)?
